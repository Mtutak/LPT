About Us

Lifestyle Personal Training was started in 2002 not only as a fitness organization but also as a way to help clients find better overall health through exercise, nutrition, and positive thinking. Our Personal and small group training can be geared towards beginners, guide clients reach specific goals or help an athlete achieve the next level of fitness for their particular sport. We offer in home training for your convenience where we provide all the necessary equipment to accomplish your goal without the need for an expensive gym membership. Not interested in training at home? Come visit us at our studio for all your training needs!
